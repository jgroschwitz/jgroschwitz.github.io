About me

I am a Mathematician, AI researcher and Data Scientist. I am currently looking for work in the Netherlands as a Data Scientist / Data Analyst! I'm based in Utrecht but don't mind a commute.

My recent work has been academic, in the field of computational linguistics, where we study how computers can process and generate human language (think ChatGPT). From 2022 to 2024, I was a recipient of a DFG Walter Benjamin fellowship for a project on neurosymbolic graph-to-text generation at the Universities of Edinburgh and Amsterdam. In 2020, I obtained a Joint PhD in Linguistics and Computer Science from Saarland University (Saarbrücken, Germany) and Macquarie University (Sydney, Australia). My Bachelor's and Master's degrees are in Mathematics, with a focus on probability theory and discrete mathematics.
